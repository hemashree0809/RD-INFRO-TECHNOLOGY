SIDD AHMED

CEO of Vdart | Visionary Leader

About VDart

VDart is a global digital talent and consulting firm founded in 2007 by Sidd Ahmed in Alpharetta, USA. What began as a one-person company has now grown to over 4,000 employees across 10 countries. VDart specializes in digital transformation, talent solutions, and proprietary SaaS and AI platforms. The company has been ranked among the Top 50 staffing firms in the U.S. and is on track to reach $500 million in revenue by 2027.

About Sidd Ahmed Sir

Sidd Ahmed, born in Tiruchirappalli, India, is a visionary leader passionate about uplifting communities, building inclusive workplaces, and bridging opportunity gaps.From humble beginnings as the youngest of six siblings, Sidd's journey took him from selling educational software in India to founding VDart in the United States.

His leadership has earned him global recognition:

  • EY Entrepreneur of the Year (2017)
  • NMSDC MBE of the Year (2019)
  • Times of India Leader of Change (2023)

Core Values

Sidd leads with the motto "People, Purpose, Planet", focusing on transforming lives, promoting diversity, and giving back to his hometown and beyond.

Impact & Vision

Through VDart, Sidd Ahmed has generated over 10,000 global job opportunities, established a "Tiruchi-first" policy to support local talent, and actively promoted sustainability and diversity in business.
